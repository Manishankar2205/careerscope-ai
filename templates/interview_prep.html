💡 Pro Tips for Success
  • Research the company culture and recent news before interview
  • Practice STAR method: Situation, Task, Action, Result
  • Prepare 3-4 questions to ask the interviewer
  • Review your resume and be ready to explain every project
  • For technical rounds, practice coding on whiteboard/paper
  • Dress professionally and arrive 10 minutes early
  • Follow up with thank-you email within 24 hours
